Topo lines — move your mouse over each to feel the reactivity

Sparse contour lines on empty space — like an actual topographic map. Three mouse styles to try.

ambient only

A — Ambient only

Contour lines drift slowly like wind over a landscape. No mouse interaction — pure atmosphere. Clean and minimal.

B — Cursor raises a peak

Where you hover, terrain rises — contour lines bunch up tightly around your cursor like a summit forming. Move away and it slowly subsides.

C — Ripple through contours

Fast mouse movement sends a wave that warps the contour lines outward. Lines stretch and compress as the wave passes through them.

D — Reveal on hover

Lines exist everywhere but are nearly invisible. Your cursor illuminates them with a soft glow — like holding a light above a relief map in the dark.
