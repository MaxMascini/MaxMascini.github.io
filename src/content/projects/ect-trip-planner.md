Systematic planning and research for a 360 km thru-hike of Newfoundland's East Coast Trail with my mother. Involved analysing 26 trail sections, coordinating three food drops by car, mapping terrain elevation profiles, and building daily distance plans accounting for weather windows and coastal exposure.

The hike took 20 days and was completed in October 2024. Read about it on the [blog](/blog/east-coast-trail/).
