My primary research focus at Dalhousie's Faculty of Computer Science. This work sits at the intersection of cognitive science and AI ethics — examining how people build mental models of AI systems (theory of mind), and what those models mean for responsible AI design.

The project involves literature synthesis, study design, and qualitative/quantitative analysis of how users describe and interact with AI agents. Supervised through the MCS program at Dalhousie University.
